Lasagna

Description

This is Allrecipe's world's best lasagna recipe.

It's a great meal to feed a family and still have leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Lasagana noodles
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to instructions
  2. Cook italian sausage and ground beef
  3. Pour in tomato sauce and add tomato paste to the meat
  4. Simmer for 2 hours
  5. Layer noodles with a layer of ricotta cheese and the meat sauce between each layer
